FAQs

  • What projects can be done on HYPEIT?

    Collaborations related to affiliated and influencer marketing are promoted through our platform. Collaborations between individual content creators is also encouraged.

  • Is there any comission fee to platform while having a deal ?

    No, Hypeit doesn't takes any comission fee as of now.

  • How can I cancel a deal?

    You have to request the collab cancellation from the other party. Mentioning a valid reason is mandatory. Platform has no responnsibility about cancellation of deals. But more cancellation would decrease your rating.
